About Us

Overview

This solution provides a motor vehicle fraud solution. The technology will allow monitoring of motor vehicle assets using data from multiple government agencies to respond to motor vehicle fraud. This solution aims to track and monitor vehicles at risk of fraud, addressing the inability to detect fraudulent activities quickly. The solution will provide daily and weekly monitoring to protect against fraud threats related to titles, customs exports, impound, and theft activity with the monitored vehicles.

Motor vehicle fraud is a $8.4 Billion problem on an annual basis, and there is no monitoring solution available on the market today. Nor does any other company have the ability to provide the data for monitoring as being provided.

Objectives

  • Reduce the incidence of motor vehicle fraud.
  • Provide real-time monitoring and alerts.
  • Integrate data from various government agencies for comprehensive fraud detection.
  • Enhance the efficiency of fraud detection and response.
  • Provide the exclusive largest and most up-to-date insight into fraud trends by state, port, vehicle, or type of fraud.

Fraud Monitoring

  • Daily Data Integration from Multiple Government Agencies
  • Monitoring and Tracking of Motor Vehicle Assets
  • Instant Access to Single Motor Vehicle Searches
  • Notifications of Activity on Monitored Vehicles
  • Integration with Customs, Export, Theft, Title, and Impound Data
  • Fraud Detection Algorithms and Alerts
  • Dashboards and Visualization of Fraud Trends
  • Customer Motor Vehicle Report Management
